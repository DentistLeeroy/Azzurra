Food

With the roots in Italy - there is a strong focus on maintaining the authentic Italian taste, where the Emphasis is placed on a proper handling of the imported ingredients and a preparation that recreates the taste of how "Mamma" made it.

Who are we?

Good food is food made from scratch from good ingredients. The better the ingredients the less cooking is required. Although the restaurant here is more internationally oriented, it is and will continue to be the traditions and fundamental cornerstones of Italian country cuisine that apply.

The menu contains a wide range of dishes created from quality products, produced and adapted to our kitchen.
